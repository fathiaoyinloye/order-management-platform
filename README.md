# Order Management Platform

A full-stack Order Management Platform built for a real import-based business by following industry-standard software engineering practices.

The project is designed to demonstrate the complete software development lifecycle—from business analysis and system design to backend development, frontend implementation, quality assurance, and deployment.

---

## Project Objectives

* Digitize the order management process for small businesses.
* Reduce manual order handling through phone calls and WhatsApp.
* Provide customers with a seamless ordering experience.
* Support inventory management and product variants.
* Manage customer payments and order tracking.
* Showcase software engineering best practices.

---

## Features (Version 1)

* Customer registration and authentication
* Product catalog
* Product search
* Product categories
* Product variants (size, color)
* Multiple product images
* Inventory management
* Product interest registration
* Pre-arrival product reservations
* Shopping cart
* Order management
* Multiple payments per order
* Payment proof upload
* Delivery management
* Order tracking
* Customer notifications
* Product returns for faulty items
